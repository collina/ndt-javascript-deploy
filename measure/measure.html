About Measurement Lab

M-Lab provides the largest collection of open Internet performance data on the planet. As a consortium of research, industry, and public-interest partners, M-Lab is dedicated to providing an ecosystem for the open, verifiable measurement of global network performance.

Open Source

M-Lab's measurement tools are openly licensed and operated, allowing third parties to develop their own client-side measurement software.

Public Data

All data collected via M-Lab is made available to the public, allowing researchers and anyone else to build on a common pool of network measurement information.

Global

Providing an open, verifiable measurement platform for global network performance.

Trusted

M-Lab's infrastructure and datasets are used by public organization, academic researchers, regulators and companies around the world.
